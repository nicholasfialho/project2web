Portfolio

One of my designs features a custom logo for an outdoor clothing brand, where I combined bold, earthy colors and rugged shapes to reflect the brand's adventurous spirit. The logo captures the essence of nature and outdoor exploration, emphasizing strength and durability. Another project I worked on is a poster design inspired by The Weeknd's music. I used vibrant, energetic visuals and abstract elements to convey the mood of his sound. The design incorporates neon lights and geometric shapes, mirroring his futuristic aesthetic and powerful stage presence. Lastly, I created a stunning mountain poster, focusing on the serene beauty of nature. I used a minimalist approach with a soft color palette to evoke a sense of calm and awe. The mountains were rendered with intricate details, emphasizing their grandeur and natural majesty, while the overall design communicates peace and adventure.
